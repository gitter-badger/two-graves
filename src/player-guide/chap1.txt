== The Nation of {nation_name}

[role=full-image]
image::https://res.cloudinary.com/rwdalpe/image/upload/v1438498433/seorsus-opt_v01daf.svg[]

Welcome, and thank you for your interest in the {product_title} adventure path! 
This adventure will take you through the nation of glossterm::[{nation_name}], a 
nation of opportunity and a paragon of the virtuous, civilized world. From its 
highest peaks to its deepest caverns, you will become embroiled in the nation's 
mysterious past, the lives of its people, and the shaping of its future.

In this guide you will find information about glossterm::[{nation_name}] and its 
major settlments that any individual who lives in or has briefly researched the 
nation would know. Feel free to use these sections to help build your characters 
and integrate them into the world.
